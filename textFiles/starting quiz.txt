Your knowledge on ancient Egypt will be tested by questions. If you do
not feel comfortable beginning the quiz, please review the lesson. Once
you have begun the quiz, you must complete all the questions, there is no
time limit. When you click an answer, the correct response will appear in
green, while the others in red. Then, after 3 seconds, the next question
will appear. In the top right corner, you will see your score. Press the
Start button to begin. Good luck!
