Egypt was ruled by 31 different dynasties.
Dynasty: A succession of rulers from the same family
Divided into 3 main time periods over a span of 3000 years.
              The old kingdom 2700BCE, middle kingdom 2200BCE
The middle kingdom 2100BCE - 1800 BCE
The new kingdom 1500BCE - 1070 BCE
The Pharoah ruled Egypt with absolute power 
and were considered gods. The Pharoah was in charge of 
overseeing all the lands, creating laws, and taxes.
