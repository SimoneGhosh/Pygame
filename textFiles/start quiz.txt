Your knowledge on ancient Egypt will be tested by questions. If you do
not feel comfortable beginning the quiz, please review the lesson. Once
you have begun the quiz, you must complete all the questions, there is no
time limit. When you clock an answer, the correct response will appear in
green, while the others do not change. Then, after 3 seconds, the next
question will appear. You will be able to view your score at all times.
Press the Start button to begin. Good luck!
