You are living in 1400BCE and hear a rumour about a treasure chest
filled with a billion dollars worth of jewels hidden in a pyramid
chamber. You decide to go on a mission to find the jewels but beware!
There is a venomous snake that lives in the pyramid that will kill
you if you make it angry or otherwise. The snake is the most agitated
by one thing: wrong answers. So be confident in your knowledge before
you begin! The questions will test your knowledge (from the lesson),
decision-making skills, and a bit of luck. Complete all the challenges
successfully to reach the chest alive!
